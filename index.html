Education

I attended the University of central Florida for about four years pursuing
a degree in Digital Media on the Game Design track. In my studies I worked
in various fields I studied fine art, computer science, coding, and design.
all culminating to my game design upper level courses.

In these courses I worked on various teams developing games and software
from scratch. Typically I worked as the lead artist or design lead but
In these smaller settings it was necesarry for us to branch out and contri-
bute to various sections of the development process. In the section below I detail
some of my past projects.

Robot Rigatoni

In the near future where old service droids are set out to pasture, one robot
decides to hunt bounty on the Martian Dune. Armed with plasmalero pistols he is
ready to challenge any opponenet in his way. "Robot Rigatoni" aims to blend sci fi
and western aesthetics while maintaining a playful tone. Using common tropes, vibrant
colors, and creative character design to blend characteristics of each genre.

I worked as the lead artist on this project as well as creative director. We had
a team of four people and it took us about four months to develop. It was tailored
to work on an arcade cabinet. So the controls and so forth are tailored towards
joysticks and multiplayer play.

Career

I worked for a contracted period as a motion graphics artist for Convention News
